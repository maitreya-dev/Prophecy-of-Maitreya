The Future Buddha

According to ancient Buddhist texts, Maitreya will be the future Buddha of this world. He currently resides in the Tusita heaven, awaiting the right time to appear on Earth. The prophecy states that he will emerge in a time when the Dharma has been forgotten, bringing renewed spiritual awakening to humanity.

Signs of the Coming

• The lifespan of humans will extend to 80,000 years • The oceans will recede, revealing ancient treasures • A great wheel-turning king named Sankha will arise • The world will enter an era of peace and prosperity • Wisdom and compassion will flourish among all beings

The Great Assembly

When Maitreya appears, it is said he will hold three great assemblies under the Nagavriksha tree. Those who gather will attain enlightenment through his teachings, bringing forth a new golden age of Dharma understanding and spiritual realization.
